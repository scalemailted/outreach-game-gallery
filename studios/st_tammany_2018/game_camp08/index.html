About Our Games

Our games are weird and downright unexplainable. However here is a little background story. In our Dodger game, a friendly and derpy looking fish tries to escape the grasp of a lover shark that just wants to go on a date with the fish. On the other hand, our Platformer game includes two levels and is about how angel version of Gary the snail tries to get back home to Spongebob, his loving owner. On his way back to him, Gary will go on the comeback journey of his life. That's about all we can explain so have fun playing our games!
