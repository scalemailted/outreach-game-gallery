About Our Games

In our game "Space Escape" you are a spaceship flying through the astroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. While this is going on our next game takes place, "Robot Recall". In this game you are a robot who has been captured by the spaceship seen in "Space Escape". Your original creators initiated a recall on all robots they had created, including you. You must run and jump through the spaceships already broken rooms and try to make it out, all gears and springs attached.
